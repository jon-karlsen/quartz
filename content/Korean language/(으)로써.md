The grammar `(으)로써` expresses a means or method by which an action or situation has occurred, and is equivalent to "by means of", "by", or "through" in English. This grammar answers the question of "How?" or "By what means?" something is done. It is most frequently used in formal or literary contexts. 

> 그는 노력으로써 선공했다.
> _He succeeded by effort._
