| Technique | What | When | Time | Space |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Two-pointer | Traverse data structure using two pointers | Sorted arrays, finding pairs | `O(n)` | `O(1)` | 
| Tortoise & Hare | Fast and slow pointers | Linked lists, cycle detection | `O(n)` | `O(1)` |
| Sliding window | Optimise sub-array sums | Contiguous subarrays, substrings | `O(n)` | `O(1)` or `O(k)` |
| Two-pass | Iterate twice | Several passes are required | `O(n)` | `O(1)` |
| Cyclic Sort | Sort by placing elements in correct spots | Elements in range `[1, n]` need sorting | `O(n)` | `O(1)` |
